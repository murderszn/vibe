Example Learning Week

Concrete Grade 7 STEM example week for the OpenTutor workflow.

Weekly Timeline

Monday

Student opens weekly assignment README in GitHub, reads goal/rubric/deliverables, and asks Vibe for clarification in Discord.

Tuesday

Student completes research notes, Vibe asks guiding questions, and student commits notes to the repo.

Wednesday

Student builds a model/notebook/diagram/explanation and asks Vibe for debugging or concept support.

Thursday

Student revises based on rubric, parent reviews draft, and student writes short reflection.

Friday

Student submits final artifact, parent reviews final work, and student adds project to portfolio folder.
